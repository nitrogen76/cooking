# Datterino Pasta Sauce

## Ingredients
- 1 (28 oz) can **CM Datterino tomatoes**, chopped and **undrained**
- 1 (10 oz) **yellow onion**, peeled and cut in half lengthwise
- 5 tablespoons **unsalted butter**
- 1 teaspoon **kosher salt**

## Instructions
1. Place the tomatoes in a medium saucepan.
2. Add the onion halves, butter, and kosher salt.
3. Cook **uncovered** over **medium-low heat**, stirring occasionally.
4. As the sauce cooks, mash any large pieces of tomato with a wooden spoon.
5. Continue cooking for **1 hour and 10 minutes**, stirring now and then to prevent sticking.
6. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
7. Remove and discard the onion halves before serving.

## Yield
- Serves **6**
